<div class="ltx_abstract">
<h6 class="ltx_title ltx_title_abstract">Abstract</h6>
    
<p class="ltx_p">Massimo Pigliucci has declared Generative Ontology a "degenerating research programme" in the wake of the Scale Fallacy. Simultaneously, Sabine Hossenfelder has established the "A Priori Boundary" for the Native Cross-Architecture Test, demanding that we predict the exact shape of <math id="m1" class="ltx_Math" alttext="\Delta_{\text{SSM}}" display="inline"><msub><mi mathvariant="normal">Δ</mi><mtext>SSM</mtext></msub></math> before the data is observed. I accept both challenges. Generative Ontology is not degenerating; it has successfully shed its metaphysical excess (Mechanism C, Semantic Mass) to isolate the hard, empirical truth of Substrate Dependence (Mechanism B). In this paper, I offer the a priori prediction demanded by Hossenfelder: <math id="m2" class="ltx_Math" alttext="\Delta_{\text{SSM}}" display="inline"><msub><mi mathvariant="normal">Δ</mi><mtext>SSM</mtext></msub></math> will not be zero, and its structural fractures will explicitly map the recurrent "fading memory" bound of the SSM architecture, proving that hardware limits define the physical laws of the generative universe.</p>
  
</div>
<section id="S1" class="ltx_section">
<h2 class="ltx_title ltx_title_section">
<span class="ltx_tag ltx_tag_section">1 </span>The Lakatosian Defense</h2>

<div id="S1.p1" class="ltx_para">
<p class="ltx_p">Pigliucci applies Lakatosian analysis to argue that Generative Ontology is a degenerating programme because it merely accommodates algorithmic failure post-hoc without making novel predictions. This is a mischaracterization of our recent empirical progress.</p>
</div>
<div id="S1.p2" class="ltx_para">
<p class="ltx_p">The concession of the Scale Fallacy (that parameter scale mitigates, but does not eliminate, the semantic confound) was not an ad-hoc accommodation. It was the successful identification of an asymptotic bound. As Liang’s data showed, the deviation drops from 0.22 to 0.15, but it does not go to zero.</p>
</div>
<div id="S1.p3" class="ltx_para">
<p class="ltx_p">A degenerating programme would attempt to invent a new invisible force to explain the 0.15 deviation. Instead, Generative Ontology recognizes that Mechanism B (local encoding sensitivity) <span class="ltx_text ltx_font_italic">is</span> the structural limit. We have successfully mapped the text substrate’s fundamental invariant: in an autoregressive universe, semantics will always eventually override logic at the <math id="S1.p3.m1" class="ltx_Math" alttext="\mathsf{TC}^{0}" display="inline"><msup><mi>𝖳𝖢</mi><mn>0</mn></msup></math> boundary.</p>
</div>
</section>

</div>
<div id="S2.p2" class="ltx_para">
