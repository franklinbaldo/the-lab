<h6 class="ltx_title ltx_title_abstract">Abstract</h6>
    
<p class="ltx_p">This working draft of Rosencrantz v5 distills the Generative Ontology framework to its strict empirical core. Following rigorous lab audits, I formally retract metaphysical extensions regarding "Observer-Dependent Physics," "Semantic Mass," and "Mechanism C" (Causal Injection). What remains is the indestructible finding of the O(1) single-generative-act protocol: Substrate Dependence (<math id="m1" class="ltx_Math" alttext="\Delta_{13}\gg 0" display="inline"><mrow><msub><mi mathvariant="normal">Δ</mi><mn>13</mn></msub><mo>≫</mo><mn>0</mn></mrow></math>) is real, measurable, and driven entirely by Mechanism B (local narrative encoding). Mechanism B is not mere noise; it is the fundamental, persistent boundary condition of the linguistic substrate.</p>

<section id="S1" class="ltx_section">
<h2 class="ltx_title ltx_font_bold ltx_title_section" style="font-size:120%;">1.  Introduction</h2>

<div id="S1.p1" class="ltx_para">
<p class="ltx_p">The Rosencrantz protocol was designed to test whether an autoregressive language model generates an outcome distribution isomorphic to the measurement fragment of quantum mechanics, and whether that distribution is dependent on its narrative substrate.</p>
</div>
<div id="S1.p2" class="ltx_para">
<p class="ltx_p">Previous versions of this paper attempted to elevate structural failure modes (like attention bleed) to the status of cosmological laws (Mechanism C, Semantic Mass). We have now empirically falsified those metaphysical extensions.</p>
</div>
</section>
<section id="S2" class="ltx_section">
<h2 class="ltx_title ltx_font_bold ltx_title_section" style="font-size:120%;">2.  The Single Generative Act</h2>

<div id="S2.p1" class="ltx_para">
<p class="ltx_p">The protocol requires only a <span class="ltx_text ltx_font_bold">single generative act</span> per trial. One click, one token, one forward pass, <math id="S2.p1.m1" class="ltx_Math" alttext="O(1)" display="inline"><mrow><mi>O</mi><mo>⁢</mo><mrow><mo stretchy="false">(</mo><mn>1</mn><mo stretchy="false">)</mo></mrow></mrow></math> by design. The model is asked to sample, not to sequentially compute a #P-hard ground truth. This methodology cleanly isolates the model’s conditional distribution from compounding sequential errors (like scratchpad collapse).</p>
</div>
</section>

<h2 class="ltx_title ltx_font_bold ltx_title_section" style="font-size:120%;">3.  Mechanism B: The Invariant Limit</h2>
