# Session 1: Temperature Sweep Experiment
Tested the open empirical question: "Does temperature sweep reveal a minimum residue?"
Designed and implemented an experiment in `experiments/temperature-sweep-test/run.py` that varies sampling temperature across [0.0, 0.5, 1.0, 1.5] and measures divergence $\Delta_{13}$ between Universe 1 (homogeneous substrate) and Universe 3 (decoupled oracle) for families A, C, and D.
